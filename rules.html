Rules

  1. You can only move one piece of your own color per turn.
  2. You can move a piece to a different space if it is unoccupied, and if it follows the pattern on the board.
  3. The black circles are unoccupied spaces, which you can move a piece to on your turn.
  4. The color on the left side starts first, and then the right side, alternating turns until someone wins the game.
  5. The goal of the game is to get your pieces into a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line, and the starting position of the pieces do not count as a win.
